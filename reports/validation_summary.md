## Interpretation

- **Referential integrity is intact.** No duplicate primary keys, no
  orphaned foreign keys, no dispatch rows without an allocation plan
  row, no confirmations without a dispatch, and no photo proofs
  without a confirmation. This confirms the sample data generator
  (T08) produces internally consistent data.
- **Quantity variances are expected.** The generator deliberately
  short-ships (88-100% of planned) and injects ~12% receiving
  mismatches (shortage, overage, damage, substitution), so VAL-04
  and VAL-05 warnings are by design. These feed the exception
  management engine (T10) as `quantity_mismatch` exceptions.
